FAQ

What activities can Spin track?
Spin tracks your activity across:
  • Cycling/ spinning
  • Running/ walking
  • General sport (e.g., basketball, soccer)
How do I get set up?
You can set up Spin in 3 easy steps:
  • 1. Snap your wheel speed monitor onto your bicycle spokes or the disc of your spin bike
  • 2. Slap your foot speed monitor onto your leg
  • 3. Download the free Spin app onto your iPhone and hit sync
Can I sync with my Nike FuelBand, FitBit or Jawbone Up?
Spin can spit out data in any format you like. Spin tracks 5 core metrics:
  • Calories
  • Distance
  • Cadence (RPM)
  • Speed (average, max)
  • Time
Beyond these metrics, Spin can calculate your Nike FUEL burned, to easily add it on to your total if you use a Nike FUEL band.
